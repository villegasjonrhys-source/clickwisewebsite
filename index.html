Introduction

In today’s digital generation, social media has become part of students’ everyday lives. While it allows communication and access to information, it also brings problems such as fake news, cyberbullying, and misuse of personal data. Many students are not fully aware of the consequences of irresponsible online behavior.

Our mission is to educate students to become responsible digital citizens by providing reliable information and practical tips. Our vision is to create a school community where students use social media safely, respectfully, and responsibly.

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to raise awareness among students about responsible social media usage. This campaign encourages students to think critically before sharing information, avoid cyberbullying, and protect their personal information online.

Description of the Project

The ClickWise project is implemented through this website as an information platform. It provides educational content such as tips, reminders, and awareness information about digital responsibility. Students can learn how to identify fake news, practice respectful communication, and protect their privacy online.

This website serves as a digital campaign platform where students can access reliable and easy-to-understand information. The goal is to promote safer and more responsible social media use within the student community.
